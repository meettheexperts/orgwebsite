Responsive Layout

Look:

A responsive layout adapts to the screen size. On larger screens, content might be spread out, while on smaller screens, it stacks or rearranges itself to fit. Elements resize and reposition fluidly.

Feel:

Responsive layouts offer a seamless experience regardless of the device. They are user-friendly and prevent the need for excessive zooming or scrolling. The content feels like it was designed specifically for the device you're using.
