Full-Screen Layout Example

Full-screen layouts use the entire screen to showcase content, often featuring large images, videos, or typography. They may lack traditional navigation menus and rely on scrolling for interaction.

Full-screen layouts can feel immersive and impactful. They draw users' attention to the central content, making a bold statement. Scrolling is a key part of the navigation experience.
