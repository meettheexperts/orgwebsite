Grid Layout Examples with Bootstrap

Look:

Grid layouts use a structured grid system to organize content. Elements are typically aligned in rows and columns, creating an orderly and organized appearance.

Feel:

Grid layouts offer a structured and visually pleasing design. They provide a sense of order and make it easy for users to scan and find content.
